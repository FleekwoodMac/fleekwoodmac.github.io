Best Cars

"Best Cars" is a fictional national car dealership. A market survey suggested a centralized nationwide database for dealership reviews. I was tasked with creating a feature enabling customers to find branches by state and access reviews. Customers can also register, submit their own reviews for any branch.

Backend Services

Cloud functions were developed to oversee dealers and reviews, with Django models and views employed for car make and model management. Additionally, integration of Django proxy services and views was executed.

User Management

User management was established utilizing the Django user authentication system, while continuous integration and delivery were also configured.

Watson Natural Language Understanding Service

All product reviews authored by authenticated users are stored in Cloudant. The cloud application employs the NLU service to assess the sentiment of user-posted review comments. Positive tones result in a green happy smiley icon, neutral tones manifest as a yellow version, while negative tones evoke a red frowning icon within the review interface.
