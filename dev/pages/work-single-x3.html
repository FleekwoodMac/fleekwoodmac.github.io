Streamlining & Boosting Conversions

Marketing efforts within a subscription model rely heavily on efficiently activating customers and capturing leads. Gateway pages are utilized to direct users seamlessly from ads to non-searchable landing pages. When I assumed responsibility for the gateway system, it was quite archaic and cumbersome. Multiple pages were constructed for different screen sizes, and products were hard-coded, lacking personalization. This led to significant bottlenecks, wasted hours, and a disconnect between ad creative and landing page content. Recognizing this issue, I developed responsive Bootstrap templates. I also engineered a solution for utilizing automated product algorithms. Additionally, I introduced more personalized features from ads to lead and member capture. This initiative resulted in a substantial increase in conversions as ad and landing page content became more aligned. Consequently, this endeavor saved countless hours and alleviated challenges for developers, designers, and marketers, greatly satisfying many colleagues. This marked the beginning of my journey and fascination with full-stack development, driven by my desire to address challenges with code and creative problem solving.

Conversion Lift

Now that marketing funnels display products aligned with ads and personalized to the audience, media spend became more efficient, resulting in an average conversion increase of 30%.

Automated Products

Initially, products were all hand-selected by marketers based on data reports. While there was a solution for dynamic best sellers, there were no products specifically aimed at converting new customers based on ad performance. To address this, I conducted research and devised a method to leverage available data in a dynamic format, automating this process and saving time.
