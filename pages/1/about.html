Hi I'm Maggie!

I started my journey in the field of design, honing my skills to create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces. Along the way, I realized the importance of a seamless user experience, prompting me to dive into front-end development. Learning JavaScript and React opened new doors as I transitioned from design to actively building dynamic web projects within my role. As I gained confidence, I realized my passion extended beyond front-end work. This realization led me to make a pivotal decision: to transition into full-stack software development. With determination, I embarked on a comprehensive course, earning certifications that solidified my expertise. Having completed my studies in June, I'm now eagerly seeking opportunities in the realm of full-stack software development, excited to contribute my diverse skill set and passion for crafting end-to-end solutions.
