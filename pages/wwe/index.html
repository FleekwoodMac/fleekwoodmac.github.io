Calling All Women Entrepreneurs!
The COVID-19 pandemic disproportionately impacted women in the workforce, but with a little extra support, you can still grow your business during this challenging time.  We invite all female-identifying entrepreneurs, whether you are just starting out, planning to start in the future, or are looking to grow your business, to join us as our speakers provide the tools to take your journey to the next level.     
  Two separate dates, same great event!
Featured Speakers
Pam Tarr
Founder of Ace Entertainment
Pam is the founder of Ace Entertainment, which focuses on creating strategic alliances between the worlds of advertising and entertainment to achieve systemic global change. With a career built on the development and nurturing of creative talent, projects, and unconventional ideas, Tarr consistently creates new business models where she has facilitated the blossoming of non-profit organizations, brilliant young directors, trade associations, and non-traditional marketing strategies.
Rachel Hood
Accredited Financial Coach and Licensed Master Social Worker
As a member of Operation HOPE for the past five years, Rachel has run programs focusing on credit, homeownership, and small business.  Currently, Rachel coordinates programming for an 8-week program that helps women-owned businesses by providing them with training and networking.
Michele Morris
Founder of Blessingway Enterprises
Michele combines storytelling with marketing strategies to bring forth the strengths of her clients and projects. Her innate ability to see connections where none is obvious, her leadership qualities and proclivity for storytelling have served her clients well. As an award-winning producer of commercial campaigns for some of the top brands in the world - Coca-Cola NASCAR, Toyota, Microsoft, Procter & Gamble, Google, and more she honed her expertise in crafting a clear message and strong, effective branding.
Judy Kessler Blok
Financial Services Professional
With a degree in Business Management from Pepperdine University, Judy has served as the CEO of the Encino Chamber of Commerce and started her own non-profit organization. She has also worked in marketing, research, and the financial industries for both Fortune 500 companies and small businesses. She is currently a Financial Professional with NYLife Securities, LLC, providing employee benefits for small businesses and retirement planning for business owners.

About 50/50 Leadership
In 2004, Pauline Field founded 50/50 Leadership to alter the perception of women leaders and their underrepresentation.
50/50 Leadership provides a wide range of online financial literacy and training programs for women at various life stages and circumstances across the US, including those who have been in foster care, incarcerated, survived domestic violence, and/or who have experienced homelessness.
With its 150+ volunteers, 50/50 Leadership is a 501(c)(3) non-profit committed to empowering educating, and inspiring women from all walks of life to step up into their full leadership potential.
